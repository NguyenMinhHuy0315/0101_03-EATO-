Truffle-infused Wagyu Delight

Our signature Wagyu striploin is seared to a perfect crust and finished with a silky truffle butter reduction, letting the marbling speak for itself. Served alongside Yukon gold mashed potatoes and a whisper of roasted garlic confit, it's the dish our regulars order again and again — and the one we're proudest to put our name on.
