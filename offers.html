How EATO Rewards Works

A simple points system that turns every meal into your next reward — no cards to carry, no fees to pay.

1. Sign Up

Create a free EATO Rewards account online or at the host stand in under a minute.

2. Dine & Earn

Earn 1 point for every $1 spent on food and beverage, tracked automatically to your account.

3. Redeem Rewards

Turn points into dining credit, complimentary dishes, or exclusive experiences whenever you're ready.

4. Level Up

Reach point milestones to unlock higher membership tiers and even better perks.
